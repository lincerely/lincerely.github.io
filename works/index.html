Your Face Your FateEvaluate your fate through face reading with computer vision

Your Face Your Fate is a project to recognize face features through photos.

The concept of this project is from traditional Chinese fortune telling. It can predict the fortune and future of a person by the shape of his face, eyes, nose, mouth etc.

Dlib is used to detect facial feature and libSVM is used to classify their genre.
